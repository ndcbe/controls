Schedule and Course Organization (old 2021)#

Getting Started#

CBE 30338/32338 Chemical Process Control will be conducted as an in-person course for the 2022 Spring semeter. Please use the period before the start of the semester to prepare your systems and technology. Pay attention to the following the following details:

  • A major component of the course is hands-on use of real hardware to implement working control systems. You will need to purchase an individual copy of the Temperature Control Labortory available through Amazon and apmonitor.com. You will need the device no later than the first Friday of the first week of class.

  • The Temperature Control Lab uses a USB-A port. If your laptop is a newer model with only USB-C ports then you will need to purchase a USB-C to USB-A adaptor (such as this for an Apple Mac), or USB-C docking station (like this from LENTION).

  • Course materials will be available through this repository and from open-source materials avaiable throught the web.
